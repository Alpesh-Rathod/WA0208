What We Bring

The Directors of Edge Property Group have extensive experience in site consolidation, feasibility analysis, project funding and all aspects of sales and marketing delivery. We have overseen the successful marketing and sale of over $10bil worth of residential homes across the range of housing types: land, house and land, and premium apartments. We tend to work with Developers who are ambitious, organised and who take pride in their product.

The volume of our sales over the past three decades provides us with an unmatched understanding of buyer expectations relevant to the stock you are taking to market in that location. We have a proven ability to devise the right strategy for each individual project. We know how to drive a sales and marketing campaign, how to keep all parties up to speed, and how to assess its progress, to shift gears in either direction to break through our targets.

DEVELOPERS - WHAT WE OFFER

We help Developers prepare their project to go to market, to present it for maximum exposure, and price it for optimum margins. Our approach is to understand your goals for the project, then draw on our experience and expertise to devise a customised strategy through our proven research and technologies , that takes into consideration all market factors.

We offer an end to end service, choosing and preparing a team that is committed and responsible for the life of the project. We handle all enquiries, through our technology platform deliver all aspects of sales and after-sales service through to completion. Campaigns Of A Premium Look & Feel

Our one-stop service relieves you of the burden of managing multiple suppliers when your team needs to stay focused on the big picture. In addition to a premium brochure, website and EDMs, we have excellent relationships with the leading real estate websites and print publications, allowing us to negotiate individual rates, deals and placements. We create inviting display homes where buyers can touch and feel the finished product, and have a calendar of PR and community events that organically increase exposure and interest in your project.

A key to your success is our ability to match your project with the right sales agents in your market. We have tested networks of agents in different communities, which we arm with a strategic release strategy allowing for price escalation at each stage. A comprehensive review of each stage takes place before the launch and the prices are monitored throughout the campaign. Our digital systems provide real-time market updates, project updates and buyer feedback to ensure homes are sold at the optimum time and for the best price.
